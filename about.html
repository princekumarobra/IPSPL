About Intelligent Property Solutions

Delivering leak-free & long-lasting waterproofing for 15+ years.

Our Journey

Intelligent Property Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a Gurugram-based waterproofing and construction company with over 15 years of experience in delivering high-performance, long-term leak-free solutions for residential, commercial and industrial structures.

We specialise in PU/APP membranes, cementitious coatings, retaining wall waterproofing, terrace/balcony waterproofing and advanced civil protection systems.
