Privacy & Data

This Privacy Policy describes how Intelligent Property Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and discloses personal information when you use our website or contact us.

1. Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry, request a quote, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number and email address.
  • Location / project address and optional message or requirements.
  • Any photos or documents you voluntarily share to help with quotes or diagnosis.

2. How we use your information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide quotes or site visits.
  • Schedule inspections and coordinate service delivery.
  • Send service-related updates, warranty information and support.

3. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third parties when necessary to provide services (for example, where we use a subcontractor for on-site works), or where required by law.

4. Cookies & analytics

We may use basic cookies and analytics tools to understand site usage and improve the website. Cookies do not contain identifying personal data. If you prefer, you can disable cookies in your browser settings — note that some site features may then be limited.

5. Data retention

We retain enquiry and contact details as long as necessary to respond to requests and for legal or warranty record-keeping. If you ask us to delete your details, we will remove them except where retention is required by law.

6. Your rights

You can request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask for corrections, or request deletion where permitted by law. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
