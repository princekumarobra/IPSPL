Our Services

We provide durable, warranty-backed waterproofing & construction solutions using branded materials and trained teams. Below is a detailed list of services we deliver with timelines and typical scope.

What we do

Basement & Podium Waterproofing

PU membrane systems, cementitious tanking, drainage prep and positive-side solutions for basements and podiums. Includes moisture checks and leak testing.

  • Surface prep & repair
  • PCC layer / protection screed
  • PU membrane application & testing
Terrace & Balcony Waterproofing

Flexible PU coatings, elastomeric membranes and protective screeds for terraces & balconies to prevent infiltration and cracking.

  • Primer & PU topcoat
  • Joint & detail sealing
  • UV protective finishes
PU / APP Membrane Systems

Full roof membrane solutions, APP torch-applied membranes where needed and mechanical protection layers to ensure longevity.

  • APP membrane installation
  • Geotextile & protection board
  • Heat/UV protection
